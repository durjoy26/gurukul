Tips to follow the routine

  • Keep phones away during meditation and revision blocks to reduce distraction.
  • Use short 2–5 minute breathing breaks between long study sessions at university.
  • Adjust sleep time if your classes or commute require it — maintain 6–8 hours of high quality sleep.
